Honeymoon

Phulchowki is nestled amidst the lush green landscape of Kathmandu, Nepal. This place is the highest point in the Kathmandu Valley rim, offering panoramic views and various flora and fauna. This place is one of the preferred destinations amongst all the places in Kathmandu. It’s a perfect place for bird watchers, nature lovers and travellers alike. Whether you are seeking a tranquil escape or adventure, this place perfectly blends adventure and scenic views. Moreover, nestled within this natural paradise lies the esteemed Phulchowki temple, a site of great spiritual significance. Phulchowki promises an unforgettable journey with its pristine heights and breathtaking scenery..
